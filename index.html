Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start using College Buddy?

Simply click on the "Start Exploring" button or navigate to the Chat Assistant page. You can then type your questions in natural language, and our AI will provide helpful responses.

What kind of information can I get?

You can get information about campus locations, department details, faculty information, hostel facilities, canteen timings, library resources, sports facilities, and much more.

Is College Buddy available on mobile devices?

Yes! College Buddy is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

How accurate is the information provided?

Our AI is trained with the most up-to-date campus information and is regularly updated to ensure accuracy. However, for critical information, we recommend verifying with official college sources.

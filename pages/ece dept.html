ECE HOD Cabin

Location: Third Floor, A Block

The ECE HOD Cabin is situated on the third floor of A Block. It serves as a dedicated space for the Head of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering. The cabin is designed to facilitate departmental meetings, administrative work, and discussions with students and faculty.

Your guide to using Tara and College Mantra effectively

Introduction

At SRIT, we understand how important it is to have seamless access to information. That's why we've introduced two powerful assistants to guide you through your journey. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or visitor, our virtual assistants—Tara and College Mantra—are here to make your experience more efficient, interactive, and enjoyable.

Tara – Your Virtual Voice Assistant

Tara is designed for interactive voice-based assistance, allowing you to stay on the go without needing to type. Perfect for multitasking or hands-free interaction!

Key Features:

  • Voice Responses: Ask Tara anything, and it will answer you verbally.
  • Instant Assistance: Get quick answers about locations, schedules, events, and more.
  • Hands-Free Interaction: Perfect for when you're multitasking.
  • Personalized Help: Tara adapts to your preferences over time.

Sample Voice Commands:

  • "Open website of SRIT?"
  • "Can you open faculty profile?"
  • "Open student login page?"

College Mantra – Your Chat Assistant

College Mantra is perfect for when you prefer to type your questions and get fast, precise answers. From campus resources to event updates, College Mantra can do it all!

Key Features:

  • Text-Based Interaction: Type and receive instant, accurate responses.
  • Quick Access to Resources: Get direct links to portals, schedules, club details, and more.
  • Event Updates: Stay in the loop with latest college events and deadlines.
  • Student Services: Access fee payments, academic resources, and more with ease.

Sample Text Commands:

  • "Find location of library?"
  • "Open Toastmasters club?"
  • "Can you open exam portal?"

How to Choose Between Tara and College Mantra?

Feature Tara College Mantra
Interaction Type Voice-based Text-based
Best For On-the-go, multitasking Detailed information, links
Response Format Audio responses Text with links and resources
Use Cases Quick queries while moving Deep research, resource access

Choose Tara when you need voice responses and hands-free assistance.
Choose College Mantra when you prefer to type for quick information and access to resources.
